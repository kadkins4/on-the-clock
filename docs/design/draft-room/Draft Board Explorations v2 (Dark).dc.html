THE COLOR RULES
Matched from your screenshots, with one discipline added: orange is brand & actions only, green means live / on the clock, gold means it's your pick — and position hues are the only other color allowed on the board.
WHAT CHANGED FROM V1
Cream paper → layered near-black panels (page, panel, card — three elevation steps). Vintage slab → Archivo for display, IBM Plex Mono for data; Barlow Condensed stays for dense board labels. Position tints flipped to dark-mode equivalents at the same six hues.
HOW TO REVIEW
Same drill: the sticky control bar resizes the league (6–14), switches snake / linear / auction, and runs the pick sim. Tap any filled cell or row for the player card. Vintage v1 is still in the project for side-by-side comparison.
